# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Javo Elizalde (solo admin user): content creator and chef, manages the site from his phone on the go — after events, between shoots, on the road. Needs to add posts, experiences, and products fast without friction. Guests visiting the public site: food lovers, potential clients, event buyers browsing from mobile.

## Product Purpose

Javotecocina.com is the digital home of Javo Elizalde — NOA chef, content creator, and event producer. The public site sells digital recipe books, showcases events and brand collaborations, and drives event inquiries. The admin panel is Javo's backstage tool for managing all content without touching code.

## Brand Personality

Raw, fire-driven, authentic. Dark and bold — not polished SaaS. Feels like the back of a parrilla at night, not a startup dashboard.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (Notion, Linear aesthetics)
- Pastel food-blog warmth (cream/linen backgrounds)
- Over-animated "premium" tool vibes

## Design Principles

1. Mobile-first always — the admin is used from a phone between events
2. Friction-free ops — add content in under 30 seconds, no confusion
3. Dark and focused — the tool recedes; the content is what matters
4. Consistent with the public brand — same tokens, same feel, just functional
5. Errors are guides, not dead-ends — always tell the user what to do next

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. No specific known needs beyond standard contrast and tap-target sizing (min 44px) for mobile use.
